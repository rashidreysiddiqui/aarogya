About us

At Aarogya Rekha, we work towards a common goal of spreading awareness regarding various diseases, their symptoms and precautions one can take, so that together we all can promote better and quality living conditions for all.

If we take today’s picture into consideration, we come across the fact that many people are ill but they are not aware of it, which may affect their health conditions in coming future. To avoid such a scenario, we organize several health awareness campaigns both on local and global level to spread knowledge and information so that one can make informed decisions when it comes their health.

Founded in the current year viz., 2020, we plan to go a long way from beginning our services in Dehradun, a city in Uttarakhand, India to other cities and states of India and establishing a place in global market as well.

We hope you enjoy our services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or suggestions, please don’t hesitate to contact us.

OUR VISION

Aarogya Rekha will always strive to be the most client focused, credible & comprehensive home health-care solution providers.

OUR MISSION

Aarogya Rekha will fulfil its vision by forming an efficient, scalable and self-sustaining user service delivery model focused on its “clients centricity” at the very core of it. Delivering credible outcomes always, for its every client, every time.
